GradHire Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

GradHire ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the GradHire mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use GradHire, we may collect:

  • Your uploaded resume or resume text
  • Job-related inputs such as selected job descriptions
  • Basic technical information required to operate the app and backend services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information to:

  • Analyze resumes for job matching
  • Provide AI-powered resume optimization suggestions
  • Generate improved resume outputs and downloadable files
  • Improve app performance and reliability

3. Data Storage

GradHire does not currently require account creation. Uploaded resumes and related job-matching inputs may be processed temporarily through backend services in order to provide app functionality.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

GradHire may use third-party infrastructure and APIs to support app features, including resume processing, AI-powered optimization, and job search functionality.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

GradHire is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
